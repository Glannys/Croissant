Croissant

Croissants, those crescent-shaped agents of chaos, are like the ninjas of the bakery world. They lure you in with their golden, buttery allure, promising a simple breakfast experience. But beware! With each bite, a flaky storm ensues, turning your neat morning routine into a 'crumb'-tastrophe. You end up wearing more croissant than you eat, looking as if you've been in a light-hearted tussle with a pastry pillow. Despite the aftermath, every crumbly encounter leaves you smiling, secretly planning your next flaky mission.
